# Employee Retention & Risk Dashboard (Power BI)

## About
An HR analytics dashboard built in Power BI that goes beyond standard attrition reporting by scoring currently active employees on their likelihood of leaving, using a custom weighted Risk Score.

## Features
- KPI cards: Total Employees, Attrition, Attrition Rate, Avg Age, Avg Salary, Avg Tenure, Estimated Replacement Cost
- Attrition breakdown by Education Field, Age Group, Salary Slab, Years at Company, Job Role, Gender
- Custom Risk Score (DAX) — flags employees as High / Medium / Low risk based on overtime, satisfaction, tenure, salary hike, and work-life balance
- Dedicated Risk Analysis page with department-wise risk comparison and a drill-down table of high-risk employees
- Interactive filters by Department

## Dataset
IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition dataset (Kaggle)

## Tools Used
Power BI Desktop, DAX, Power Query
